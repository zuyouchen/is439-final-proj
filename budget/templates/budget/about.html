What is EZ Budget App?

This simple web application is an online budgeting proof of concept. In theory, as a financial planning business, you'd want to manage your advisors, clients, their budgets, incomes, and expenses in a single application. Additionally, as a client, you could login and view your budgeting information relatively easily.

The application currently just allows for superusers to perform CRUD operations on all objects. Eventually, the application could transition to a more complex authorization scheme where individual users have access to their information, advisors have access to their respective clients, and administrators have access to all.

The data in this application is fictional. Some names are taken from Suits.
